Does privacy matter?

What is end-to-end encryption?

End – to – end encryption is the best kind of encryption, so far, for encrypting the messages which are being transmitted among users. End-to-end encryption ensures only you and the person you're communicating with can read or listen to what is sent, and nobody in between, not even the messaging app. This is because with end-to-end encryption, your messages are secured with a lock, and only the recipient and you have the special key needed to unlock and read them.
